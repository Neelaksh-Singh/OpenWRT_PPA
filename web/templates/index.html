About Freifunk

Freifunk (German for: "free radio") is a non-commercial open grassroots initiative to support free computer networks in the German region. Freifunk is part of the international movement for a wireless community network.

  • Building a large-scale free wireless Wi-Fi network that is Decentralized.
  • Building a backbone network based on microwave transmission over longer distances.

Technologies

Freifunk uses mesh technology to bring up ad hoc networks by interconnecting multiple Wireless LANs.

Goals

Reducing the digital divide along with unhindered distribution of knowledge and resources
